How to play Gameplay Objective
Control Player 1:
Use ← ↑ ↓ → keys on keyboard
Control Player 2:
Use W A S D keys on keyboard
Extra controls:
Press the T key on the keyboard to switch turns
Press the P key to pause the game
Each player can move 120px on each turn
Battle starts when both players touch
In the battle each player will choose a weapon
The goal of this game is to gather the most powerful weapon and beat the other player in battle
The players start with a strength of 25
The strength is increased by gathering powerups:
Star: +3 strength, Cake: +5 strength, Bread: +7 strength, Laser: +10 strength
